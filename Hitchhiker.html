The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

Disclaimer- this is a series of four books, but I only read the first three. For now. The final one is on my to-read list.

This is an awesome book. It's the first fiction book I've read in ages, and it's fantastic. Firstly, it creates a whole new perspective on the world. When the mice said "We more or less ran your planet for the last fifty years" I stopped and realised that it's actually true. The outcome of most experiments on Earth depends on mice, determining our knowledge of the universe. The translator fish inspired inventors to build an earbud that live translates the language it hears from the microphone into your ear in your own language. I found this book while looking at Elon Musk's bookshelf via vc.ru, and I agree with him that this is a must-read.

10 Outta 10
