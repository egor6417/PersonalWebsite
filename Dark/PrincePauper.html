Prince and the Pauper

Prince and the Pauper appealed to me because it is set in 19th century London. I enjoyed the setting so much, that the arrogant Prince stopped annoying me after a while. The plot is predictable, but the way the author implements the subplots was amazing. For example, I was really worried when Pew threw the Prince and the Prince's friend into prison, but that was solved quickly enough that I wasn't tired of it, but still worried.

9.125 OUTTA 10
