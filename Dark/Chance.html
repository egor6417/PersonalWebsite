Chance by Michael Brooks

I really like the way this book was written. It is a collection of essays by scientists, stitched together by Michael Brooks. This way, a book gave me a lot perspectives on the same thing. As a mathematical freak, my favourite chapter was on casinos and how to win. Did you know that if you find two bookies that have different odds, you can bet so that you get a small win whatever the outcome. However, the book was boring to read at times (when it touched upon biology and chemistry) but that's just me.

7.875 OUTTA 10
