Think Smart by Richard Restak.

Think Smart was a fascinating entry into brain science (neurology). It had a lot of different interesting facts. For example, the number of neurons in the brain is biggest in infantry and smallest in the eighties and nineties, but the number of connections between them increases as we take part in different activities.To allow your brain to function at its best, you have to:

  1. Eat the Mediterranean Greek diet
  2. Drink red wine
  3. Exercise for at least three times for forty five minutes a week (walking will do)
  4. Take part in different activities

9 OUTTA 10
